Salmon with Garlic Lemon Butter Sauce

toreglia22nd March 2021 at 6:44pm
Live recipe salmon

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • butter

What to do

  1. Oven at 375 degrees F / 190 C
  2. melt the butter, add some lemon juice, mix in the garlic
  3. pour it over the salmon
  4. bake for 8 - 12 minutes
