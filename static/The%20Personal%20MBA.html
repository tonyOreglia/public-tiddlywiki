Chapter 1: Value Creation

  • Five Interdependent Processes of Every Business
    1. Value Creation: Creates and delivers somethign of value
    2. Marketing: Others want the creation
    3. Sales: People will pay the price for the creation
    4. Value Deliverty: the creation satisfies expectations
    5. Finance: There is enough profit for the business owners to continue
  • Iron Law of the Market

    if you don't have a large group of people who want what you have to offer, your chances of building a viable business are very slim.

  • Core Human Drives – The Drive to ...
    1. Acquire - physical assets
    2. Bond - acquire love and respect
    3. Learn - satisfy curiosity
    4. Defend - ourselves and loved ones
    5. Feel - sensory stimulation
    • There is also a strong drive to increase social status. People are extremely atttentive to social status.
