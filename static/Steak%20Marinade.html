Steak Marinade

toreglia9th January 2021 at 1:24pm
Live recipe

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ⅓ cup fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 ½ tablespoons garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoons dried basil
  • 1 ½ tablespoons dried parsley flakes
  • 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • Can also add hot sauce and/or dried minced garlic

What to do

Blend it. Put it with the meat. Refrigerate up to 8 hours.
